ATS Optimization Tips

Recommended

  • Use standard section headings (Education, Experience, Skills)
  • Include relevant keywords from the job description
  • Use simple, clean formatting
  • List skills explicitly in a dedicated section
  • Quantify achievements with numbers and metrics
  • Use standard fonts: Arial, Calibri, or Times New Roman
  • Save as PDF or DOCX

Avoid

  • Tables and text boxes for layout
  • Headers or footers for important information
  • Images and graphics for core content
  • Unusual or decorative fonts
  • Multi-column layouts for critical sections
  • Keyword stuffing or invisible text
  • Abbreviations without spelling them out first
